Welcome to the Austin Startup Games

What's it all about?

The Austin Startup Games return to center stage at Austin Music Hall on Saturday, January 25, 2014 at High Noon CST. Austin's Second Largest Startup Event (and largest "athletic event" for geeks) brings together 15 Austin Startups and countless hackers, hustlers and hipsters to benefit charities throughout Austin.

The structure of the Startup Games is simple: Each team represents a charity and the team who wins the Games donates the most to their charity. How do you win the games, you ask? Simple. There are 10 (geek approved) athletic events where teams participate. Each win, show and place for a startup gains points and the team with the heighest average points at the end wins.

The history of the Startup Games dates back to Ancient Greece and the inception of Gods and Men. Or 2011 at uShip World Headquarters. Our historian is a little fuzzy on some details.

Austin Startup Games started in 2012 by 8 Austin startups who wanted to give back to the Austin community in a fun and a creative way. After talking, drinking and debating what to do they finally came to a consensus and that's how Austin Startup Games was born. As one founding member stated - "Let's have a day of fun games that show the skills of our employees. We can raise money to donate to the charity of our choice and have the bragging rights that WE are the Champions!"

We have now grown Austin Startup Games to hosting 16 startups in this half-day competition featuring ping pong, foosball, darts, shuffleboard, flip cup, beer pong, giant Connect4, Pop-A-shot and trivia. Plus, the CEOs of each company compete in the final game (which is always a surprise and not revealed until moments before the game is to commence). Last year it was a mechanical bull, what do you think we will put these CEOs through this year?!?

Who will become Champion of Austin Startup Games 2014?!?! Come and see for yourself.

The Games

What games are being played?

10 Events: Beer Pong, Connect 4, Darts, Flip Cup, Foosball, Mystery Event, Ping Pong, Pop-a-Shot, Shuffleboard, Trivia Points awarded as follows:

  1. 5 points for 1st place in each game
  2. 3 points for 2nd place in each game
  3. 1 point for 3rd place in each game (in the event where 2 teams advance to a championship round, 3rd & 4th place teams will both be awarded 1 point each).
  4. Each event is worth the same amount of points.
  5. In the event of a tie, 1 game of Rock, Paper, Scissors will be played (shoot on 3, first player to throw a winning hand wins)
  6. Awards will be given to overall 1st, 2nd, & 3rd place companies.

Where is all this going down?

The ATX Startup Games is brought to you by a group of dedicated volunteers. They are developers, entrepreneurs, designers, marketers and everything in between. They have one thing in common, they want to give back to the Austin, its startup scene and all the amazing people that make this city great.
